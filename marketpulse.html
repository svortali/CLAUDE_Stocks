Sector Highlights
NRC releases modernized SMR licensing framework — 18 months faster

The Nuclear Regulatory Commission published a draft "risk-informed, technology-neutral" review pathway for sub-300MW advanced fission reactors. The new framework could compress the standard 8–10 year licensing timeline to 5–6 years for well-prepared applications. This is the most significant regulatory development for OKLO and NNE in two years — the licensing drag has been the primary bear case for both names since IPO.

Bullish — OKLO, NNE, nuclear sector
Blackwell GB200 shipping at volume — AI chip tightness persists into 2027

TSMC and NVIDIA confirmed Blackwell NVL72 systems are shipping at volume in Q2 2026, but demand continues to outpace supply by 12–18 months. Data center operators are pre-ordering capacity through 2027. This sustains the AI infrastructure capex cycle across every layer of the stack: PLTR (more enterprises can afford AIP deployments), RXRX (inference costs for biology AI are falling), and semiconductor names broadly.

Bullish — AI infrastructure ecosystem
JOBY completes Stage 4 FAA conformity — type certificate back on 2026 track

Joby Aviation cleared Stage 4 conformity inspections in April, leaving one final stage before type certification. The company reaffirmed a 2026 type certificate target and holds 3,400+ pre-delivery reservations from Delta Air Lines. Separately, EASA certified a European eVTOL competitor — validating the regulatory pathway and the category more broadly. Stage 5 flight testing is underway.

Bullish — JOBY

IonQ deployed its first 64 algorithmic qubit system through AWS and Microsoft Azure Quantum in May — three quarters ahead of published roadmap. The 64 AQ threshold is where quantum advantage over classical compute starts to appear in narrow domains (quantum chemistry, combinatorial optimization). Revenue is still pre-commercial-scale, but consistent roadmap execution is exactly the bull case: IonQ is the only public company reliably hitting quantum performance milestones.

Bullish — roadmap credibility established
