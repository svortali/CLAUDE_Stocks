Investment Criteria

We focus exclusively on sectors exposed to structural megatrends — technological shifts, demographic tailwinds, and policy catalysts that compound over a 5–10 year horizon regardless of short-term market cycles. Selection criteria: addressable market above $500B by 2035, meaningful public market access via liquid equities, early-to-mid adoption curve with asymmetric upside relative to downside risk, and defensible competitive moats (IP, network effects, regulatory barriers, or cost advantages). We exclude commodities, mature consumer industries, and sectors where differentiated alpha is structurally hard to achieve.
