Why Now

  • NATO spending commitments — European members accelerating to 2%+ GDP; new procurements benefit US and allied defense primes
  • Ukraine/Taiwan geopolitical backdrop has permanently reset defense budget baselines across the West
  • AI in defense — autonomous systems, C2 software, cyber are attracting non-traditional players (Palantir, Anduril, Shield AI)
  • Cybersecurity is a structural growth market independent of macro; breach frequency and severity increasing YoY

Key Risks

  • Continuing resolutions and budget delays in US Congress create lumpy revenue for defense primes
  • Traditional primes (RTX, LMT, NOC) face cost-plus contract margins that limit upside — more bond-like than growth
  • Cybersecurity consolidation compressing margins as large platforms commoditize point solutions
  • Geopolitical de-escalation (unlikely near-term but possible) would reduce urgency of defense budgets

Catalysts to Watch

  • NATO summits and allied defense budget announcements
  • US NDAA appropriations process (FY2027)
  • CrowdStrike and Palo Alto earnings — ARR and net new ARR growth
  • Anduril, Shield AI, or Joby Aviation IPO timelines (private — comps re-rate on news)
